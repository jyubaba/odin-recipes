Mung Bean Noodle Soup

aka Noodle Soup in Glass Tupperware

Easy meal prep for when you're craving something with noodles and soup. Just add the pre-made sauce, water and microwave -- then eat :)

Ingredients

  • mung bean noodles
  • soft tofu
  • bok choy
  • cabbage
  • green onions
  • cilantro
  • choice of cooked protein
  • garlic
  • soy sauce
  • spicy bean paste
  • mirin
  • chicken bouillon
  • fried chili oil with peanuts
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Add one bundle of mung bean noodle, chopped boy choy and cabbage, and tofu into the glass tupperware.
  2. Cut up your choice of cooked protein into small cubes and place them inside.
  3. Sprinkle with green onion, black pepper, and cilantro on top before closing the glass tupperware.
  4. To create the sauce, add 6 cloves of minced garlic into a bowl.
  5. Add one soup spoon of each the following into the sauce bowl: soy sauce, mirin, spicy bean paste, chicken buillon, and fried chili oil with peanut.
  6. Mix up sauce well and store inside fridge until ready for use.
  7. When ready to make the noodle soup, add two spoonfuls of sauce into the glass.
  8. Next, add water into the mixture until nearly full. Then, proceed to microwave for 5 minutes.
  9. Enjoy!
