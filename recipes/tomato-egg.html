Tomato Egg

Best served with white rice

Great to eat for dinner and save for leftovers for a hearty meal the next day :)

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe tomatoes
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups of white rice
  • garlic
  • soy sauce
  • fish sauce
  • black pepper
  • green onions
  • onion powder
  • (Optional)spam
