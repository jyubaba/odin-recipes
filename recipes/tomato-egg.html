Tomato Egg

Best served with white rice

Great to eat for dinner and save for leftovers for a hearty meal the next day :)

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe tomatoes
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups of white rice
  • minced garlic
  • soy sauce
  • fish sauce
  • black pepper
  • chopped green onions
  • onion powder
  • avocado oil
  • (Optional) diced spam

Steps

  1. Wash and start cooking your rice.
  2. Crack the eggs into a small bowl and beat for about 30 seconds before setting aside.
  3. Dice the tomatoes into smaller pieces and set aside.
  4. Grab a frying pan and put on medium heat. Once it's hot enough, pour the eggs in. Stir and cook until 80% cooked before putting it back into the small bowl and setting aside.
  5. Add 1 teaspoon of black pepper on top of the semi-cooked eggs.
  6. Using the same pan, turn the heat to high and add garlic and the diced tomatoes. Make sure to smash and squish the tomatoes for extra sauce.
  7. Add two splashes of fish sauce and three splashes of soy sauce. Next, sprinkle some onion powder and mix.
  8. Once the tomatoes have become soft and mushy, add your egg back in along with green onions (and optional spam).
  9. Cook for 2 more minutes on high and then turn off. Move it to a not hot surface.
  10. Serve with rice. Save extras with leftover rice for meal prep.
