Miso Soup

Healthy soup packed with nutrition to start of the day strong!

Great to have for breakfast or just as an addition to any meal of the day :)

Ingredients

  • cut silken or soft tofu
  • chopped green onions
  • dried cut seaweed
  • miso paste

Steps

  1. Measure 500mL of water (or dashi soup base if you prefer) and leave it on the stop on medium heat.
  2. Once the soup is boiling, add your cut tofu and sprinkle some cut seaweed.
  3. Turn off the stove and add a spoonful of miso paste and mix it in thoroughly into the soup.
  4. Sprinkle some cut green onions on top before serving.
